About Dr. OP Singh

OP Singh Classes, led by Dr. OP Singh, is a renowned educational institute that has gained international recognition for its exceptional coaching and philanthropic initiatives. Dr. OP Singh, a distinguished educationist and philanthropist, holds an MSc (Hons.) and a Ph.D. from Panjab University, Sector 14, Chandigarh, which he completed in December 1993. After obtaining his Ph.D., he began his career as a lecturer in the government sector while simultaneously serving the coaching industry in Chandigarh and Panchkula.

Dr. OP Singh initially started his career as a tutor in individual subjects. Recognizing the need for comprehensive coaching for medical and non-medical entrance exams, he assembled a team of highly qualified tutors specializing in these areas. What sets his institute apart is their expertise in providing coaching to droppers/repeaters, a specialized batch catering to students who require additional support and guidance.




The team at OP Singh Classes excels in teaching the complete courses for classes +1 and +2, with a strong focus on subjects like botany and zoology. Their proficiency extends to preparing students for NEET and other competitive examinations. They have developed an online platform for test series, enabling students to assess their knowledge and improve their performance in NEET, medical representative courses, and MSc entrance tests in zoology and botany.

Over the years, OP Singh Classes has successfully nurtured thousands of medical and para-medical experts who have gained admission to prestigious institutions in India and abroad. The institute's dedication to education has been recognized by the Department of Higher Education, Government of Himachal Pradesh, which has endorsed their courses under the Medha Protsahan Yojna for a duration of five years.

In addition to their outstanding academic contributions, Dr. OP Singh and his team have been actively involved in philanthropic endeavors. For the past thirteen years, they have provided free coaching to three thousand underprivileged students from government schools in Himachal Pradesh at their center in Panchkula. Their commitment to uplifting the underprivileged extends beyond Himachal Pradesh, as they have also taught hundreds of girl students in the Kalka constituency from 2014 to 2019.

One of their recent initiatives involved providing free crash courses for NEET to a hundred girl students from the Meritorious School in Punjab. This endeavor aimed to level the playing field and empower these students to pursue a career in the medical field.

Furthermore, Dr. OP Singh and his team have adopted numerous underprivileged students from slum colonies and villages in Panchkula who attend government senior secondary schools. These students are offered various job-related medical courses during the academic year 2023-2024, enabling them to acquire valuable skills and pursue meaningful careers. Dr. OP Singh and his esteemed team have made significant strides in assisting students with their career choices after completing class 10. Recognizing the importance of making informed decisions regarding academic streams and career paths, they have collaborated with child psychologists, career counseling experts, and industry managers to develop a groundbreaking application called "Intellitest."

Intellitest is a cutting-edge application designed to provide psychometric analysis to students who are currently in or have recently completed class 10. This app aims to help students understand their aptitudes, interests, and personalities, which are crucial factors in determining the most suitable academic stream for their future endeavors. Looking ahead, Dr. OP Singh envisions implementing additional initiatives to support the educational needs of disadvantaged students. With his visionary approach and passion for education, he continues to develop plans that will create a positive impact on the lives of students in need.

OP Singh Classes has earned a global reputation for its exceptional coaching, philanthropic efforts, and commitment to empowering students from all walks of life. Through their expertise, dedication, and visionary leadership, they are transforming the lives of countless individuals and contributing to the betterment of society as a whole.
