About Dr. OP Singh Classes

Panchkula Educational Associates, also known as OP Singh Classes, is an educational institute located in Sector 15, Panchkula, India. The institute is registered with the Micro, Small, and Medium Enterprises (MSME) government of India under the Udyam registration number UDYAM-HR-13-0011078. This registration signifies that the organization falls under the MSME category and is eligible for certain benefits and support provided by the government.

Panchkula Educational Associates operates in the educational support services sector, which is classified under the national industry classification code 85. This sector primarily involves providing various types of support services to the education sector. The institute offers coaching classes for students in the higher secondary level, specifically for plus one and plus two. Additionally, they provide coaching for entrance examinations, helping students prepare for competitive exams such as those required for admission to universities or professional courses.




Furthermore, Panchkula Educational Associates has been empaneled with the government of Himachal Pradesh under the Medha Protsahan Yojna for a period of five years. This association implies that the institute has been selected as an approved coaching center and is eligible to provide educational support services to students under this government scheme.

The organization also offers job-oriented training courses that are affiliated with the government of India. These courses are designed to equip students with the necessary skills and knowledge required for specific job roles, enhancing their employability in the workforce.

Panchkula Educational Associates has established partnerships with various educational non-governmental organizations (NGOs) such as Jan Shikshan Sansthan. These collaborations indicate that the institute actively engages with these organizations to deliver educational initiatives and programs that benefit the community.

Additionally, the institute has associated with corporate houses, which suggests that they collaborate with companies in various ways, such as offering training programs or internship opportunities to students, fostering industry-academia connections.

The organization is also involved in the Swavalambvi Bharat Abhiyan, which is an initiative aimed at serving underprivileged children in North India. Through this program, Panchkula Educational Associates extends its educational services and support to disadvantaged sections of society, helping them access quality education and opportunities.

In addition to its core educational services, Panchkula Educational Associates conducts career guidance workshops and seminars. These events provide students with valuable insights and information about different career options, helping them make informed decisions about their future paths.

Moreover, the institute actively engages in philanthropic activities, demonstrating a commitment to social responsibility and community welfare. These philanthropic endeavors may include initiatives such as scholarships, donations, or educational support to marginalized communities.

Overall, Panchkula Educational Associates (OP Singh Classes) is a registered educational institute operating in Panchkula, India. Its Udyam registration and affiliations with government schemes, educational NGOs, corporate houses, and social initiatives indicate its dedication to providing educational support services, coaching classes, job-oriented training, career guidance, and philanthropic activities to benefit students and the community at large.
